Best Tennis Academies in India

Zeeshan Ali Tennis Academy(Karnataka,Bangalore)


ZEESHAN ALI TENNIS ACADEMY is a world class tennis coaching facility headed by former Indian Davis Cupper Zeeshan Ali. ZATA at Inventure intends to offer the benefits of a tennis based sports curriculum that is carefully designed to bring out the best in every child, both on and off court.ZATA's High Performance Tennis (HPT) residential program is ideal for the motivated junior who is willing to go through a rigorous training regimen and looking to break through into a higher tournament play.

Rohan Bopanna Tennis Academy(Karnataka,Bangalore)


The Rohan Bopanna Tennis Academy (RBTA) brings a refreshing new dimension to tennis coaching in India. ​ Founded by India's leading doubles tennis star, Rohan Bopanna, the Academy is focused on enabling holistic development of athletes and grass roots development of the game.With a professional children's tennis coaches overseeing the program and special inputs from a globally qualified fitness trainer, RBTA introduces a balanced curriculum - with equal emphasis on fitness and sports skills, to young children looking to play tennis.

More on Rohan Bopanna Tennis Academy


Sania Mirza Tennis Academy(Telengana,Hyderabad)


Located in Murtuzaguda, Hyderabad, the Sania Mirza Tennis Academy (SMTA) is a leading tennis academy in the country, owned by the tennis legend herself. With one of the top international coaches from France and Sania’s own mentorship, your child will undoubtedly receive excellent training in this institution.The academy has come a long way and boasts modern infrastructure, coupled with a dedicated training curriculum in place. The institute also provides free training to talented underprivileged children.

Mahesh Bhupati Tennis Academies


The academy was a modest venture founded in 2006 but has now spread out across 20 cities all around the nation. With centers in metropolitan cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Gujarat, and more, MBTA has made a massive name for itself in tennis coaching. Founded and managed by 12 time Grand Slam winner Mahesh Bhupathi, the academy is known for its advanced infrastructure and high-quality coaching. The venture also boasts Gaurav Natekar as their CEO, a former no. 1 player in India, and a veteran Davis Cup winner.
